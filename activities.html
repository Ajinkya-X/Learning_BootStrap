Real Madrid - Frequently Asked Questions

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. Founded in 1902, it is one of the most successful clubs in European football history. The club plays in La Liga, the top division of Spanish football, and has won numerous domestic and international titles including 15 UEFA Champions League trophies.

Real Madrid's home stadium is the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, located in Madrid, Spain. With a capacity of approximately 81,044 spectators, it is one of the largest and most iconic stadiums in Europe. The stadium has been the club's home since 1947 and is currently undergoing a major renovation project called the Bernabéu Master Plan.

Real Madrid has won a record 15 UEFA Champions League titles (as of 2024), more than any other club in the competition's history. Their first Champions League title was won in 1956, and their most recent victory came in 2024. This incredible achievement showcases the club's dominance in European football over several decades.

Real Madrid has had many legendary players throughout its history. Some of the most notable include Cristiano Ronaldo (2009-2018), Zinedine Zidane, Raúl, and Alfredo Di Stéfano. Currently, the squad includes Vinícius Júnior, Jude Bellingham, Luka Modrić, and Kylian Mbappé, among other talented players.

Tickets for Real Madrid matches can be purchased through the official Real Madrid website (realmadrid.com), authorized ticketing partners, the Santiago Bernabéu stadium box office, and various official resellers. Ticket availability and prices vary depending on the match, competition, and seat location.

Real Madrid was founded on March 6, 1902. The club was originally called Madrid Football Club before being renamed Real Madrid (Royal Madrid) in 1920 after being granted the "Real" title by King Alfonso XIII. This royal designation became a symbol of the club's prestige and tradition.

The rivalry between Real Madrid and Barcelona, known as "El Clásico," is one of the most famous rivalries in sports. These matches between the two Spanish football giants have been played since 1902 and are highly anticipated events. The rivalry reflects not only sporting excellence but also cultural and regional differences between Madrid and Catalonia.

Real Madrid has won 35 La Liga titles as of 2024, making them the most successful club in the history of Spanish football. The club has dominated Spanish domestic football throughout the decades, winning their first La Liga title in 1932 and continuing to be a dominant force in the league.

The Real Madrid Foundation is a social organization created by Real Madrid to develop social programs and initiatives. The Foundation focuses on using football as a tool for social inclusion, education, and development, particularly targeting vulnerable populations. It operates globally and has implemented numerous social projects worldwide.

Real Madrid has won 20 Copa del Rey titles, making them one of the most successful clubs in the Spanish cup competition. The Copa del Rey is one of the oldest football competitions in Spain, and Real Madrid's success in this tournament further demonstrates their dominance in Spanish football across all major competitions.
